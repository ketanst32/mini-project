ABOUT

We have to create a weather forecasting website which is used for predicting future weather conditions based on current observations and historical data. It involves the study of atmospheric science and meteorology, and it plays a crucial role in our daily lives.

Weather forecasts are essential for a variety of sectors, including agriculture,Aviation, shipping, and emergency services. They help farmers plan their planting schedules, pilots decide whether to take off or land, and emergency services prepare for potential disasters.

Weather forecasting relies on a network of weather stations that collect data on temperature, humidity, wind speed and direction, pressure, precipitation, and other atmospheric variables. These data are then processed and analyzed using complex mathematical models to produce accurate predictions.
